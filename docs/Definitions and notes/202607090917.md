## What the point system is

The **point system** is the communication layer. A point is the smallest named object in the system that can receive logs, relationships, documents, updates, or resources. Your definition notebook already says that everything important can be represented as a point, including houses, people, rooms, locations, resources, agreements, events, tasks, payments, and documents.

The simple version:

```text
A house can be a point.
A room can be a point.
A person can be a point.
A task can be a point.
A document can be a point.
A payment can be a point.
```

A QR code is not the point. The QR code is the entrance. Someone scans the code, lands on the point page, and can log something there. Those logs become the raw material for history and intelligence.

## Where crypto / DeFi belongs

Crypto belongs in the **financial / market / ownership layer**.

```text
Crypto / DeFi belongs here:
- financial system
- EEU / internal unit system
- market / exchange system
- ownership / staking / capital layer
- payment / settlement layer
- proof / audit layer
```

Simple explanation:

> Crypto is how value, ownership, contribution, payments, stakes, claims, and outside capital can move through the system without relying completely on traditional banks.

But it should come **after** the real-world system is legible.

Do not start with “token.” Start with:

```text
Who paid?
For what?
Who owns what?
Who is owed what?
Who contributed labor?
What value was created?
What claim exists?
```

Then crypto can represent or settle those things later.

## Where AI / machine learning belongs

AI belongs in the **intelligence / history / coordination layer**.

```text
AI / ML belongs here:
- point intelligence
- task triage
- summarizing logs
- detecting issues
- predicting maintenance needs
- matching labor to work
- explaining money flow
- generating reports
- helping you decide what to do next
```

Simple explanation:

> AI watches the logs, tasks, payments, documents, and history of the system, then turns messy activity into useful understanding and next actions.

AI should not be the source of truth. The records are the source of truth.

```text
Records = truth layer
AI = interpretation layer
```
